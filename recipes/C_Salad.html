Chicken Caesar Salad

A fresh and crunchy Caesar salad with grilled chicken and creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

Step 1

Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper and grill until fully cooked. Slice into strips.

Step 2

Chop romaine lettuce and place in a large bowl.

Step 3

Add Caesar dressing, Parmesan cheese, and croutons. Toss to combine.

Step 4

Top with sliced chicken and serve chilled.
