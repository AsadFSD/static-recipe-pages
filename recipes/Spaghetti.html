Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A simple and classic Italian pasta dish with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

Step 1

Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

Step 2

In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic slices until golden.

Step 3

Add chili flakes and cooked spaghetti. Toss well to coat.

Step 4

Season with salt, garnish with parsley, and serve immediately.
